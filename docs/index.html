Next-Generation Stealth

NexusSU utilizes a multi-pillared architecture to provide root access while remaining completely invisible to user-space anti-cheat mechanisms:

  • Native Escalation: Grants UID 0 and full capability sets directly via execve interception—no detectable user-space su daemons required.
  • Advanced VFS Masking: Defeats modern SafetyNet and Play Integrity sweeps by dropping immutable -ENOENT responses to faccessat and vfs_statx queries.
  • Procfs Buffer Scrubbing: Bypasses aggressive banking apps by dynamically intercepting vfs_read and erasing root mount traces from /proc/self/mountinfo in-memory.
